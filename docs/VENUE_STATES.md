### Notes — Prediction Markets

**Wrapper caveats matter more than the underlying.** Coinbase Predict, Sleeper Markets, and Robinhood events all wrap Kalshi, so in theory they inherit Kalshi's CFTC footprint. In practice each wrapper adds its own gate: Robinhood blocks MD entirely (Robinhood Derivatives never obtained MD approval) and pauses NV sports; Coinbase advertises all 50 but would inherit any future Kalshi TRO. Sleeper doesn't publish its own exclusions — treat as Kalshi-identical until disclosed. **MetaMask Predict is a special case: it wraps Polymarket's offshore venue, which geoblocks the US entirely — so MetaMask Predict is effectively US-unavailable despite Polymarket having a US-licensed product elsewhere.**

**Introducing broker ≠ exchange.** OG Markets and Fanatics Markets both run on CDNA as the underlying exchange, but have very different footprints (49 vs 24 states) because the binding constraint is the introducing broker's state licensing, not the exchange's.

**Active regulatory battlegrounds (2026):**
- **NV** — Kalshi TRO extended 2026-04-03; the most hostile CFTC-market state.
- **AZ** — 20 criminal misdemeanor counts filed against Kalshi 2026-03-17; DOJ/CFTC filed countersuit 2026-04-02.
- **IL, CT** — DOJ/CFTC suing these states per filings dated 2026-04-02.
- **NJ** — Federal appeals court ruled for Kalshi 2026-04 (first appellate win).
- **NY** — Excluded from CDNA/OG/Crypto.com; Kalshi live for non-sports only.

**Items worth manual re-verification before shipping:**
- Polymarket's exact US allowlist (operator does not publish).
- FanDuel Predicts' 18-state sports list (need to hit `fanduel.com/predicts/where-can-i-trade-on-prediction-markets` with a browser user-agent; API returned 403).
- NoVig Alabama status (the source lists it in both columns).
